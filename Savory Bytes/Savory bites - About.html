Our Story

How It All Began

Founded in 2010, Savory Bites began as a small family-owned bistro with just ten tables and a dream to share our passion for exceptional food with our community. What started as a humble neighborhood eatery has grown into one of the city's most celebrated dining destinations.

Our founder, Maria Rodriguez, brought recipes passed down through generations, combining traditional techniques with innovative culinary approaches. Each dish tells a story of heritage, love, and dedication to the craft of cooking.

Today, we continue this legacy with Chef Alejandro at the helm, bringing modern flair to our classic dishes while maintaining the soulful flavors that have made us beloved by our guests for over a decade.

Meet Our Chef

Alejandro Martinez

With over 20 years of culinary experience spanning three continents, Chef Alejandro brings a world of flavors to Savory Bites. Trained in Paris and having worked in Michelin-starred restaurants in Barcelona and New York, he now leads our kitchen with creativity and precision.
